## Why pillars matter

Pillars are *what you write about*. Without them, content drifts toward whatever's trending. With them, every draft has a home and the audit ("am I drifting?") becomes possible.

The mix matters too: track roughly what percentage of posts go into each pillar. Patterns emerge over a quarter.

## Wildcards

Always reserve ~10% for posts that don't fit any pillar. New ideas emerge from outside the system. If 100% of posts fit your pillars, your pillars are too broad or you're not exploring.

## How to choose pillars (if doing this manually)

Three good questions:
1. What 3-5 topics do people specifically come to you for?
2. What 3-5 topics could you write 100 posts about without running out?
3. What 3-5 topics, if you became known for them, would advance your goals?

The pillars are the overlap of those three lists.
