# Vocabulary

> **[PLACEHOLDER — fill in your own vocabulary during `/setup content`, or by listening to yourself in standups/calls and noting what you actually say]**

Your signature phrases, recognition words, and banned ones. The discipline: signature phrases appear naturally and repeatedly; aspirational ones earn their place by being kept rare.

The best way to build this list is to **transcribe yourself in real conversations** (standups, podcasts, calls) and note the words that show up over and over. Those are your voice. Cut the filler ("um", "you know"); keep the rest.

## Banned (never use)

*[Universal banned list — keep these. Add your own.]*

- Synergy. Move the needle. Circle back. Touch base. Disrupt / disruption. Game-changer. 10x. Crushing it. Hustle culture. Thought leader / thought leadership (about yourself). Humbled to announce. Thrilled to share. Unpopular opinion. Quiet quitting. Founder mode.
- "Just shipped" (about things not actually shipped).
- **AI-coded vocabulary** (see Pre-publish gate § Anti-AI tells): delve, tapestry, navigate (abstract), leverage (verb), nuanced, multifaceted, seamless, paradigm, unlock (abstract), realm, journey (metaphor), embrace (metaphor), foster, robust (non-technical), holistic.
- **Journalistic clichés:** "the thinking goes", "as the saying goes", "in a sense", "it could be argued", "one might say", "so to speak", "if you will".
