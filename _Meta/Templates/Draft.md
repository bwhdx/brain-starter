## Pre-publish gate

- [ ] Two-Year Test — would I still be happy with this in two years?
- [ ] Mission Test — integrity, courage, consideration?
- [ ] Real Test — acknowledges the work as it is, not "thrilled to share"?
- [ ] Mate Test — warm and collegial, not cold and transactional?
- [ ] Pub Test — would I use these words in a pub, not just on a panel?

## Voice formula

- [ ] Earned — paid for this with experience
- [ ] Specific — a real moment, not a principle
- [ ] Quiet — noticing, not reaching
- [ ] Brief — every word carries weight

## Anti-AI cadence (quick check)

- [ ] Em dashes: 0 or 1, never 2+
- [ ] Mirror inversions: 0 or 1, never 2+
- [ ] Tricolons: 0 or 1, never 2+
- [ ] No wisdom-reveal openers ("What I didn't expect was...") or closers ("Turns out X was the whole reason Y")
- [ ] No journalistic clichés ("the thinking goes", "as the saying goes")
- [ ] Read paragraph 1 aloud — sounds like you, not TED-talk
