# 20 Atoms — your distilled thinking

The single most important folder in the vault. Your atomic notes live here. One idea per file. Dense linking.

## Five subfolders

- **Concepts/** — mental models, frameworks, principles. Things you use to think.
- **Beliefs/** — things you believe but don't always say. Opinions that matter to you.
- **Moments/** — specific events from your life that taught you something. Lived experience.
- **Quotes/** — things people said worth keeping (yours or others').
- **Observations/** — patterns you've noticed across work, people, life.

## What makes an atom

- **One idea per file.** If it sprawls, split it.
- **Atomic title.** A short sentence that states the idea. *"Consistency beats intensity."* > *"Thoughts on long-term effort."*
- **Three outbound `[[ ]]` links minimum.** An atom without links is a stranded island.
- **`themes:` in frontmatter.** Picks up automatically in the relevant theme map under `30 Maps/`.
- **Status:** seedling (just captured) → budding (refined once) → evergreen (densely linked, stable).

## What atoms are NOT

- Highlights from a book. (Those live in source notes — extract the *insight*, not the quote.)
- Daily-note overflow. (Daily notes can graduate into atoms during weekly review; raw daily content stays in `10 Daily/`.)
- Drafts of posts. (Those go in `50 Content/Drafts/`.)
