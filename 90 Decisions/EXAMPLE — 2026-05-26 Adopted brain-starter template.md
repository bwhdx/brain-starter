# Adopted brain-starter template

> **EXAMPLE FILE — replace with your own first decision, or keep this if it matches your actual setup decision.**

## What I'm deciding

Whether to adopt the brain-starter template as my personal knowledge management system, and on what substrate.

## What I chose

The brain-starter template as scaffolded. Obsidian vault. 10 numbered folders. Atomic notes. MOCs for navigation. State in frontmatter. Bases for views. Three-tier backup (Sync + Git + Time Machine).

## Why

- **Markdown + YAML is non-proprietary.** Not buying into a vendor's data model; if Obsidian dies tomorrow the vault is still readable by any editor.
- **Local-first.** No SaaS reliability dependency.
- **Active community + Bases is GA.** Obsidian shipped Bases in core which collapses the historical "Dataview is mandatory" lock-in.
- **The substrate is opinionated; the content is mine.** Pre-fabricated structure means I'm not designing a PKM; I'm using one.

## Alternatives considered

- **Notion** — beautiful, fast capture, terrible portability. Lock-in is total. Killed by the data-ownership argument.
- **Roam** — strong bidirectional links, weak file model. Lost to Obsidian on Bases, maturity, community.
- **Logseq** — close second. Local-first, open source. Lost on Bases-equivalent.
- **Apple Notes + Drive** — frictionless capture, zero structure. Fine for notes, not for a thinking system.
- **No formal system** — the strongest counter. Most PKMs die. Risk mitigated (not eliminated) by the 30-day shakedown plan.

## What I'm uncertain about

- Whether the daily/weekly ritual will stick. The substrate is over-engineered intentionally; the discipline is the unknown.
- Whether the atom-vs-source split survives contact with real reading.
- Whether the People folder becomes a quiet CRM or a graveyard.

## How I'll know later if I was right

By 2026-11-26 (6 months in):

- ✅ I open the brain at least 5 days a week without effort.
- ✅ The weekly review has happened at least 20 of 26 weeks.
- ✅ I've returned to at least 5 atoms I wrote earlier and they were *useful*.
- ❌ If most of the above are false: either change the discipline (not the structure) or honestly accept the brain doesn't fit my life.
