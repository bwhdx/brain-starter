# 60 People — quiet CRM (optional module)

One note per person. Becomes a relationship tracker over time.

> **Optional module.** If you don't want to track relationships in your brain, delete this folder. The rest works fine without it.

## Subfolders

Group people loosely. The groupings are for navigation; the data is the same.

- **Family/** — immediate and extended
- **Friends/** — close personal relationships
- **Team/** — current colleagues
- **Advisees/** — founders/people you help
- **Network/** — wider professional contacts

## What goes in a person note

- Context: how you know them, what they do
- Last conversation: date + one-line summary
- Follow-up: optional date for when to reach out next
- Gift ideas, important dates, things to remember
- Wikilinks to atoms/projects they're connected to

## The "follow-ups due" Bases view

`_Meta/Bases/People - Follow-ups.base` surfaces person notes where `follow_up` date has passed. Walk this list during your weekly review.

## See `_Meta/Templates/Person.md`.

## Don't pre-populate

Add a person note when you wish you had one — after a meaningful conversation, when you realize you've forgotten what someone said last time, when you want to be deliberate about a relationship. Pre-seeding 30 names you might want to track is a recipe for a graveyard.
