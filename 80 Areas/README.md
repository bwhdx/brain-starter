# 80 Areas — ongoing domains of responsibility

Areas are the catch-all for parts of life that don't have an end date. The brain ships with pre-created area folders that you can use, ignore, or extend.

## Pre-created

- **Identity/** — Who I am, Why, Mission, Values, Brand bible (the spine)
- **Health/** — running, training, sleep, energy, what works/doesn't
- **Travel/** — trips, places, notes from the road, recommendations
- **Reading/** — reading goals, year-in-review, currently-reading dashboard
- **Philosophy/** — evolving frame on how to live
- **Finances/** — personal finance, investments, runway thinking
- **Learning/** — active learning campaigns (e.g., a year on a topic)
- **Memory/** — specific cherished moments worth keeping forever
- **Goals/** — yearly, quarterly aspirations and reviews
- **Routines/** — morning, evening, weekly rhythms — what works for you

## What makes something an Area vs. a Project

- **Project** has a defined outcome and a target end date
- **Area** is ongoing, no end state, accumulates over time

## Folders cost nothing

Add more as needed. Delete what you don't use. Each area folder has a README explaining what goes there.
