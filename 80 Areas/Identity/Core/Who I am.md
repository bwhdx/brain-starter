# Who I am

> **[PLACEHOLDER — this is the integrating personal narrative. Run `/setup identity` to fill it through guided questions, or write it yourself.]**

This is the document you re-read monthly to remember who you are when drift starts to set in.

It pulls together:
- Your [[Why]] (what you want freedom to do)
- Your [[Mission]] (the how)
- Your [[Values]] (operating principles)
- Your [[Ten roles]] (optional — the roles you play in life that matter to you)

And expresses them as one unified narrative — not a bullet list, but prose. Around 1,500-2,500 words.

## How to write it (if doing this manually)

Don't try to write it cold. Instead:

1. First populate `Why.md`, `Mission.md`, `Values.md`
2. Then write 5-10 belief atoms and 5-10 moment atoms in `20 Atoms/`
3. Once you have raw material, write `Who I am` as an integration of all of it
4. Re-read monthly; refine when drift becomes visible

## What this is NOT

- A LinkedIn bio
- A list of accomplishments
- A vision statement
- Anything performative

It's an honest first-person narrative meant for one reader: future-you.
