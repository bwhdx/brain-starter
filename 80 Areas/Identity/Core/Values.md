## How to use

The three load-bearing ones are the gate. The rest are the texture. When a decision feels wrong but you can't name why, walk it through your three — usually one of them is being violated.

## Don't have too many

If your top values are 7+, you don't have values; you have aspirations. The point of three is forced choice: which three do you actually use?

## How to choose (if doing this manually)

- Look at the decisions you've made in the past year. What values were you actually operating from? (Not what you wish you were — what you actually were.)
- Look at the moments you were most proud of. What values were honoured?
- Look at the moments you were ashamed of. What values were violated?

The pattern in those answers is your real values. They may not be the ones you'd put on a LinkedIn bio.
