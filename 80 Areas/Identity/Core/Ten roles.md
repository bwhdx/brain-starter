# Ten roles

> **[PLACEHOLDER — optional. The roles you play in life that matter to you. Helps with prioritisation and self-audit.]**

A pattern from the 7 Habits and 4th Generation Scheduler tradition. Ten is the cap (could be 7-10). The roles you play in life that you want to take seriously.

## Why ten

Fewer than 7 and you're under-naming. More than 10 and the list is for show. Ten forces honest selection.

## How to use

- Weekly: walk the list. Did each role get any attention? Which got the most? Was that right?
- Quarterly: are the roles still right? Any to add, drop, rename?
- Annually: which role did you grow in? Which atrophied?

## Examples of roles to include

Friend, Partner / Spouse, Parent (if applicable), Sibling, Son / Daughter, Scholar, Athlete, Entrepreneur, Creative, Technologist, Executive, Coach, Traveller, Citizen, Builder, etc.

Pick the ones that resonate. Don't add ones you'd be performing.

## Optional: "Alive"

Some people add **Alive** as the tenth role. It captures the discipline of being fully present in your own life rather than just performing the other roles. Worth considering.
