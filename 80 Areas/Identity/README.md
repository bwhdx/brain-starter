# Identity

The spine. Who you are, what you stand for, what you're trying to be.

## Core/ — the load-bearing notes

- `Who I am.md` — the integrating personal narrative (the unifying read)
- `Why.md` — what you want freedom to do
- `Mission.md` — personal mission + horizons
- `Values.md` — operating principles
- `Brand bible.md` — full operating doc (if you do content)
- `Ten roles.md` — the roles you play in life that matter (optional)

These are templates in the brain-starter. Run `/setup` to populate them through guided questions, or fill them in manually.

## Monthly re-read

`Who I am` is one of the four monthly re-reads. It drifts because you drift. Re-reading catches the drift before it becomes invisible.

## Don't

- Don't bury identity work in subfolders. Keep it in `Core/`, flat.
- Don't perform self-reflection here. These are honest first-person notes, not LinkedIn bios.
- Don't make these documents long. The brand bible can be long because it's operational; everything else stays under one page.
